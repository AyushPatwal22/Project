Competences

Why Xtruline?

1. COMPONENT TO A SOLUTION PROVIDER

PROFILES, INJECTION MOLDING AND ASSEMBLING

Have a look at the diverse possibilities, we offer complete solutions under one roof as ready-to-install products, as per the customer requirements. We provide complete package, starting from extrusion to finished goods. Xtruline is specialized in the extrusion of technical plastic profiles for light in polycarbonate and polymethyl methacrylate materials. This guarantees the constantly high quality of our products and the highest rank of properties.



2. IN-HOUSE TOOL DEVLOPMENT

IDEAS, DESIGNING & DEVELOPMENT

Our in-house designing/tooling department is equipped with modern CAD-CAM technology. Modern machinery with CNC controlled milling and wire erosion machines enable us to produce complicated profile cross- sections in a quick and economical way.





3. CO/MULTI EXTRUSION

single profile in combination of different colours and materials

By utilizing the latest production lines with our upgraded knowledge, we are capable to extrude single, double or triple layers profiles with different polymers. In this way, you receive a profile with co-extruded/multi layers materials. Our special profiles process for the plastic extrusion is the result of our vast experience and knowledge in the extrusion industry. For example, profiles for LED lights for illumination of buildings, buses or trains and with different light designs and counters profiles. Please refer profile applications.

4. IN-HOUSE COLOUR LABORATORY

PROFILES IN DIFFERENT COLOURS

We provide you with products in transparent, translucent or opaque colour, based upon your customized requirement. Being equipped with latest spectrophotometer with special colour formulation software, our in-house colour laboratory makes an objective comparison of colours under different colorimetric aspects possible. Hence, guaranteeing the exact match.

Using the colour measuring system, we produce custom colour stripes as per the colour requirement.
